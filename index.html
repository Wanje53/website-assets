About HPEN

Founded on September 9, 2024, the Health and Planet Empowerment Network (HPEN) is a youth-led nonprofit organization working at the intersection of climate justice, sexual and reproductive health rights, and gender equality.

We believe in the power of art and youth engagement to drive social change and create sustainable solutions for our planet and communities.

Our Vision

To enhance collaboration at the intersection of climate change, health, and women's rights, especially addressing the impact of climate change on sexual and reproductive health for adolescents and youth.

Our Approach

We combine grassroot activism with artistic expression, policy advocacy, and community education to create lasting change in the areas we work.

Our Thematic Areas

We work across five interconnected thematic areas to create holistic change at the intersection of health and environmental justice.

Climate Justice & Action

Empowering communities to advocate for environmental policies and implement sustainable solutions to combat climate change.

Learn more

Sexual & Reproductive Health Rights

Promoting access to comprehensive SRHR education and services that are freely chosen, individual-governed, and free from undue risk.

Learn more

Art for Social Change

Harnessing the power of creative expression to raise awareness, challenge norms, and inspire action on critical social issues.

Gender Justice & Empowerment

Youth Engagement

Creating platforms for young people to lead, innovate, and drive change in their communities on issues that affect their future.
